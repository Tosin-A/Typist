Technique

  • Curve your fingers — don't lay them flat on the keys
  • Keep wrists slightly elevated while typing, not resting
  • Use your thumb for Space only — not your index finger
  • Press Shift with the opposite hand's pinky
  • Eyes on screen, not your hands — trust muscle memory
  • After each key, return your finger to home position
